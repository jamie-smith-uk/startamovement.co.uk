Why is this different?

What we're creating is a social enterprise for creating wealth. Our single aim is to create the maximum for the people that join us. That primarily means helping you to realise your dreams - whether that's the resolving your debt, retiring wealthy, sending your kids to university, buying your first home, moving abroad or simply just managing to cope with the changes in your life.

We've thought very hard about how this should work and the structure of the enterprise to create maximum value for the community it helps. That's why we've decided to do the following:

  • Once the platform has generated enough profit to sustain itself each year, everything else goes back to you.

  • The community invested in us will shape what we build and how we offer it. Our aim is to create value for your: have some great ideas for the future, but you do too and that's more important.

  • You pay what you think it's worth. If we're creating value for you, we hope you'll pay into the platform. If not, we'll rethink what we're doing and re-focus on you again.

  • We'll share all the details of the platform's money- we'll show you where your money goes and who gets paid for what. You'll see how it makes money and where the profits actually go.
