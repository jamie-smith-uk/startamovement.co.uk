What is it?

So, here's our plan:

We're currently building a place for grown-ups to sort out their money. Where you can get organised about your life, sort out things that need attention now and muse on things that can wait a little longer. That means building a community of people and creating tools, advice, tutorials, events and analysis for you and your journey to happiness.

We then plan to harness the power of us together to get the best from the companies and institutions that we need help from. We'll focus on bring together companies that share our values and want to help so we can offer you what's most valuable to you.

Once we've got a band of like-minded people together and a collective of valueable companies, we'd like to grow even further and start offering truly valuable platforms to help people balance debt and create investments. How that works will be truly different.
