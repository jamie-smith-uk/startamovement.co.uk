Change the World by Helping Shape the Future

Join us as help to start a movement that changes the world

We've all seen the problems in our world we see and feel it everyday. The future seems less certain than ever and you may be starting to wonder what comes next

We believe that the live in what could be described as the ‘Old World’ governed and controlled by Institutions and Corporations designed for an age that is now passing and unsuited to cope with whats coming next.

But the problems we see seem too big and too complicated for us to solve. So we shrug our shoulders and hope that somehow things will work out or that someone else will do something about it.

But you can help shape a different future. A new world where power and control operates in a another way.

This is the first tiny step towards that shared vision help us start a Movement that is diverse, open and embracing and that seeks to upload, discuss and diffuse ideas and inspiration.

At it's heart this movement stands for honesty, fairness and collaboration.

Here's what we believe:

  • This movement will create a new, better future
  • Our purpose is to create things that help the many rather than benefit the few
  • Our power comes from a shared vision driven by the collaboration, diversity and energy of it's community
  • We will find the answers by being collabortive, flexible, adaptable and creative.
  • We will be open, honest and transparent about what we do and how we do it

Where do we start?

The industrial world of Money and Financial Services is at the heart of the problems we face and recreating it into something fairer and honest is a cornerstone of our movement.

So we will create co-owned financial services entity called Happe.

It will be created and controlled by you and the all the others that participate.

We're busily working through the structure of how it will operate and what the start looks like. What we do know is that it's going to very different to what's there now. It will be our first step to changing the world.
